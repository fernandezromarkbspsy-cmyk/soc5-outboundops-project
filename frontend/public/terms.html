These terms govern access to and use of SOC 5 Outbound (“the application”), an internal outbound-operations and truck-request service.

The application is available only to personnel who have been authorized for a legitimate business purpose. Access is personal, role-based, revocable, and subject to organizational policies. A Google or password authentication account does not create a right to access the application.

2. Account responsibilities

Users must keep credentials and devices secure, use only their assigned account, promptly report suspected compromise, and sign out from shared devices. Users may not share verification codes, passwords, sessions, or accounts. Actions performed through an account may be attributed to its assigned user.

Users must provide accurate operational information and use the application only for approved outbound activities. Users must not attempt to bypass access controls, view data outside their assigned responsibilities, disrupt service, introduce malicious code, scrape or bulk-export data without approval, impersonate another person, or use information for an unauthorized purpose.

Use of information is described in the Privacy Policy. By using the application, users acknowledge that operational and security activity may be processed and retained as described there and under applicable organizational policies.

7. Suspension and termination

Access may be disabled or terminated when employment or assignment changes, authorization expires, security risk is identified, these terms are violated, or the application owner otherwise determines access is no longer appropriate. Required audit and operational records may remain after access ends.

8. Internal policies and applicable law

These terms supplement, and do not replace, applicable employment terms, information-security requirements, acceptable-use rules, records policies, privacy notices, and law. Where a conflict exists, the controlling organizational policy or applicable law governs.
