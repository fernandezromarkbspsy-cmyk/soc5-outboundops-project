SOC 5 Outbound

SOC 5 Outbound is an internal operations application for authorized facility personnel. It replaces spreadsheet-based truck-request coordination with one auditable workflow.

What the application does

Authorized users create and review truck requests, assign trucks, coordinate docking, receive operational notifications, and maintain an activity history. Access and available actions depend on each user’s assigned operational role.

Why Google account information is requested

Full-time employee users may sign in with their company Google account. The application requests only basic identity information—name, email address, and Google account identifier—to authenticate the user and match the account to the approved employee access list. It does not request access to Gmail, Google Drive, calendars, contacts, or other Google content.

This application is restricted to authorized personnel. A successful Google sign-in does not grant access unless the email address is also active in the application’s employee allowlist.
